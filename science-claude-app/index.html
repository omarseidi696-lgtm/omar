Meet Science Claude,
your curious lab partner.

A tiny app that answers science questions, explains its reasoning, and hands you a new kitchen-table experiment every day. Nothing here calls a real model — every fact and experiment is hand-picked and baked in, so it works completely offline.

Ask a question See today's experiment

Curious by design

Every answer comes with a short "why it's true," not just the fact — because the interesting part is usually the mechanism.

Shows its reasoning

Answers are tagged by field — physics, biology, chemistry, space, earth science — so you can see the shape of the idea.

No dumb questions

The kitchen-table experiments use things you already own — ice, a jar, a spoon — because that's where real curiosity starts.

Interactive

Ask Science Claude something

Pick a field, or leave it on "All," then hit ask. Science Claude will pull a question from its little brain and answer it.
